What is this platform?
This is the MSU Learning Platform, a web-based system designed for higher education academicians at MSU to share resources, communicate, and collaborate.

Who can use this platform?
This platform is designed for higher education academicians including lecturers, professors, and academic staff at MSU.

What can I do on this platform?
You can upload and share academic resources, participate in forum discussions, send messages to colleagues, view announcements, and use the chatbot for assistance.

How do I register an account?
To register, click the Register button on the login page and fill in your full name, email address, and password, then click Submit.

How do I log in?
Enter your registered email address and password on the login page, then click the Login button to access your account.

I forgot my password. What should I do?
Please contact your system administrator to reset your password as the platform currently does not have a self-service password reset feature.

How do I update my profile?
Click on your profile icon or navigate to the Profile section, then update your full name, bio, department, phone number, or profile picture and save the changes.

How do I change my profile picture?
Go to your Profile section, click on the profile image area, upload a new image file, and save your changes.

How do I upload a resource?
Navigate to the Resources section, click the Upload button, fill in the title and description, select your file, and click Submit to upload it.

What file types can I upload?
The platform supports common academic file types including PDF, Word documents, PowerPoint presentations, and other standard document formats up to 10MB in size.

How do I download a resource?
Go to the Resources section, find the resource you want, and click the Download button to save it to your device.

How do I search for resources?
Browse the Resources section and use the search or filter options to find resources by title, type, or uploader.

How do I create a forum post?
Navigate to the Forum section, click the New Post button, enter your title, topic, and content, then click Submit to publish your post.

How do I comment on a forum post?
Open a forum post and scroll to the comments section at the bottom, type your comment in the input box, and click Submit.

How do I like a forum post?
Click the Like button or thumbs up icon on any forum post to show your appreciation for the content.

What is a FAQ post?
A FAQ post is a forum post that has been marked as a Frequently Asked Question. These posts are highlighted to help users find commonly needed information quickly.

How do I mark a post as FAQ?
When creating or editing a forum post, enable the Mark as FAQ option to flag it as a frequently asked question.

How do I delete my forum post?
Open your forum post and click the Delete button. Note that you can only delete posts that you have created yourself.

How do I send a message to another user?
Go to the Messages or Chat section, select the user you want to message from the list, type your message in the input box, and press Send.

How do I view my chat history?
Navigate to the Messages or Instant Chat section and select a conversation from the list to view the full chat history with that user.

Who can I send messages to?
You can send messages to any registered academician on the platform.

Where can I see announcements?
Announcements are displayed on the Dashboard when you first log in, and you can also find them in the Announcements section.

How do I create an announcement?
Navigate to the Announcements section and click the New Announcement button, fill in the title and description, then click Submit to post it.

Who can create announcements?
Any registered academician on the platform can create and post announcements.

What is the Dashboard?
The Dashboard is the main page after you log in. It shows you an overview of recent announcements, forum posts, resources, and platform statistics.

How do I log out?
Click the Logout button in the navigation bar or header to securely log out of your account.

What should I do if I encounter an error?
If you encounter an error, try refreshing the page first. If the problem persists, contact your system administrator with details of the error.

Is my data secure on this platform?
Yes, your data is stored securely in the platform database and your account is protected by your login credentials.

Can I edit my forum post after publishing?
Yes, you can edit your forum post after publishing by opening the post and clicking the Edit button.

How do I view other users profiles?
You can view basic profile information of other academicians when browsing forum posts, resources, or the user list in the messaging section.

What departments are available on the platform?
The platform supports academicians from all departments at MSU. Your department information can be set in your profile settings.

How do I contact support?
For technical support or issues with the platform, please contact your system administrator or the MSU IT department.

What is the maximum file size for uploads?
The maximum file size for resource uploads is 10MB per file.

Can I upload multiple files at once?
Currently the platform supports uploading one file at a time per resource entry.

How do I view all forum posts?
Navigate to the Forum section to see a list of all published forum posts from all academicians on the platform.

What is the chatbot for?
The chatbot is here to help you navigate the platform, answer common questions, and provide guidance on how to use the various features of the MSU Learning Platform.

What can the chatbot help me with?
The chatbot can help you with questions about registering, logging in, uploading resources, using the forum, sending messages, viewing announcements, and general platform navigation.

How does the forum work?
The forum allows academicians to post discussions, share knowledge, ask questions, and engage with colleagues. You can create posts, comment on others posts, and like content you find useful.

Can I delete my account?
To delete your account, please contact your system administrator as account deletion requires administrative access.

How do I update my email address?
To update your email address, go to your Profile section and update the email field, then save your changes.

How do I add my phone number?
Go to your Profile section, enter your phone number in the phone number field, and save your changes.

What is my department field for?
The department field in your profile identifies which academic department you belong to at MSU, helping colleagues identify your area of expertise.

How do I find a specific user to message?
In the Instant Chat or Messages section, browse the list of registered users to find the person you want to contact.

Are messages private?
Yes, messages sent through the platform are private and can only be seen by the sender and the recipient.

How do I know if I have new messages?
Check the Messages or Chat section regularly for new conversations. The platform may show unread indicators on new messages.

Can I share resources with specific people?
Resources uploaded to the platform are accessible to all registered academicians. Currently there is no option to restrict resources to specific users.

How do I edit my resource after uploading?
Navigate to the Resources section, find your uploaded resource, and look for an Edit option to update the title, description, or file.

How do I delete a resource?
Find your resource in the Resources section and click the Delete button to remove it from the platform.
